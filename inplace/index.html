Interactive Visualization User Study

This HIT consists of a series of visual analysis tasks. In these tasks, you will be presented with a map visualization of some data. When a region of the map hovered over, it triggers a request to load data for that region, which will be loaded inplace for the region. You will be asked to read values from this visualization, which may require hovering over different parts of the map to reveal more information.

Each task consists of tracking the value across different regions of the map. You are able to freely interact with the visualization, but you should try and make a quick visual judgment to complete the task.

You will receive a $0.10 reward for each task completed. You can keep track of your accumulated reward in the "Current Reward" box. After completing all of the tasks, you will receive a $0.80 completion bonus reward. You must complete all the tasks to receive the completion bonus. If you do not wish to complete all the tasks, you can press the "Finish and claim current reward" button to exit this HIT and be paid your accumulated reward. You will only be paid once you complete all the tasks or click the finish button.

The results of these tasks will be used for a research study on visual perception for interactive visualizations. You may view our privacy policy for more information.

If you are ready to begin, please click the "Start" button below.
